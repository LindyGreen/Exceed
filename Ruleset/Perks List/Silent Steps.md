# Silent Steps

**Requirements:** Stealth 1
**Category:** Universal
**Cost:** 5 CP
**Traits:** -


## Short Description
You can sneak at full speed instead of half speed, and can choose to move at half speed for +2 bonus to stealth rolls.

## Effect
You can sneak at full speed instead of half speed, and can choose to move at half speed for +2 bonus to stealth rolls.
